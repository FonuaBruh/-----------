How it works

With the battery, you can pay with tokens without having to purchase TON from exchangers to pay blockchain fees.

Start using the battery

Update Tonkeeper on iOS and Android to version 4.3, then refill the battery in Settings â†’ Battery. After charging, the battery status will appear on the main screen.

Charging the battery

You can charge the battery using the card using built-in payments on iOS or using tones or stablecoins. Send a few dollars in stablecoins or tones to the special address battery.ton - the battery will be charged for your wallet.

Tonkeeper's battery is measured in charges, each charge covers the fee for a simple TON transfer transaction. More complex transactions may require 5, 10 or more charges.

Gifts

You can give charges to another user by entering his wallet address.
